About TradeArena

TradeArena is a Sydney-based educational paper-trading platform built for Australian university students. Members trade simulated portfolios across ASX-listed equities, US stocks, crypto and FX; complete bite-sized lessons in the School of Trading; and compete in weekly campus leaderboards. No real money is at risk on the platform at this time — see our Risk Disclaimer for full detail.

Quick facts

  • Founded: 2025, Sydney, Australia
  • Audience: Australian university students and recent graduates
  • Markets: ASX equities, US equities, major crypto, major FX pairs
  • Stack: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS frontend, Supabase backend, AWS-hosted
  • Real money at risk: none, today
