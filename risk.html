Paper trading only

Every trade you place on TradeArena is simulated. No real orders are sent to any exchange or broker, and no real money is at risk on the platform at this time.

You will be required to opt in separately, complete additional identity and suitability checks, and accept a separate set of terms before any real funds can be deposited.

Past performance

Simulated performance, paper-portfolio returns, leaderboard rankings and back-tested results displayed on TradeArena do not indicate or guarantee future performance in real markets. Real trading involves slippage, partial fills, financing costs, taxes, brokerage, currency conversion and emotional pressure that paper trading cannot fully replicate.

The lessons, reels, theses, comments, charts, signals and market data shown on TradeArena are provided for general educational purposes only. They are not personal financial product advice and they do not take into account your objectives, financial situation or needs.

Get licensed advice

Before making any real-world investment decision, you should:

  • Read the relevant Product Disclosure Statement (PDS) or Target Market Determination (TMD);
  • Consider whether the product is appropriate for your circumstances; and
  • Seek personal advice from a licensed Australian financial adviser (AFSL holder).

Market data

Quotes and charts on TradeArena come from third-party data providers and may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate. We display a "delayed" pill when a price snapshot is more than 60 seconds old. Do not rely on TradeArena prices for real-money decisions.

Crypto-asset risk

Crypto-assets are highly volatile, largely unregulated, and can lose substantial value in short periods. Many crypto-assets are not financial products under Australian law and are not covered by ASIC's investor compensation arrangements.
